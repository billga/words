Bran Flakes

2       c            Bran
2       c            Whole wheat flour
1/2   c            Nonfat dry milk powder
3       tb           Brewer's yeast (optional)
1       t            Salt
1/4   c            Oil
1       tb           Molasses
1       c            Water

Combine dry ingredients.  Make a well in the centerand add oil,molasses and water. Mix well. Divide into three parts and roll out as thin as possible on greased cookie sheets. bake in 350 F oven 15-20 minutes, or until lightly browned and crisp. If dough is not completely dry, turn oven off and let it remain longer. Break into small pieces. Store in airtight container. Makes 1 lb of cereal.
