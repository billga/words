Grape Nuts

3 1/2   c            Graham flour
1 1/2   c            Brown sugar
1       t            Salt
1       t            Baking soda
1       t            Cinnamon
2       c            Buttermilk
2       ts           Vanilla

Mix all ingredients, spread on a jellyroll pan and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Let cool overnight then break into pieces and grind in a food processor until grapenut consistency(you know, the cereal). Toast in a 250 degree oven for 35 minutes, stirring occasionally. Good on ice cream, as a cereal or just to munch on.
